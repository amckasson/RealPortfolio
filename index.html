About Me

I have a diverse work background. I've done various sales jobs, and worked in Restaurants doing both front and back of house work. In April of 2021 I wanted to make a professional change. Earlier my job in the restaurant industry had been taken by the pandemic. Though I really enjoyed what I was doing, having my job taken away was hard. I wanted to get into an industry with a little more flexibility. I then found out about Eleven Fifty Academy.

When I was accepted into Eleven Fifty Academy I chose the Software Developer path. It's focus is on teaching C#; it also goes over some HTML and CSS. That's what I used to create this page.

My goal is to use my personal and professional skills to begin my journey working in the field of technology. I love technology; learning about it through personal use and study. I think it's an incredibly exciting field with limitless opportunity. I plan on continuing my learning with more languages as well as gaining some certificates in whatever fields intrest me such as AZURE cloud services.

Thank you for stopping by and checking out my portfolio. Feel free to come back as I plan on continuing to make changes; as well as adding more projects. You can find them beside this blurb, feel free to click on them and check them out.
